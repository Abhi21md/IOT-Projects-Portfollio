TEST PROCEDURE
--------------

STEP 1 – Motor Test
• Check forward, backward, left, right.
• Adjust speed slider in Blynk.

STEP 2 – Metal Detector Test
• Bring metal near sensor.
• Forward/backward must stop.
• Left/right should still work.

STEP 3 – Auto Patrol Test
• Enable auto mode.
• Car should move forward and backward repeatedly.

STEP 4 – Radar Test
• Place object on left side.
• Left laser + buzzer must activate.
• Servo should lock position.

• Repeat for right side.

STEP 5 – ESP32-CAM
• Upload camera streaming code.
• Open Serial Monitor.
• Copy IP address.
• Open in browser.
• Verify live video feed.
